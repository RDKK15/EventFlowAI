# Purpose

The Engineering Handbook defines how BizPart AI is implemented.

While the Constitution defines the principles of the platform, the Engineering Handbook defines the engineering practices required to build and maintain the platform consistently.

Every contributor, whether human or AI, is expected to follow this handbook.

---

# Relationship to the Constitution

The Constitution defines:

• Why BizPart AI exists

• What principles govern the platform

• What architecture has been adopted

The Engineering Handbook defines:

• How code is written

• How features are implemented

• How testing is performed

• How reviews are conducted

If the Engineering Handbook conflicts with the Constitution, the Constitution always takes precedence.
